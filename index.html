The story how Leo got into our family

We are a family of 3: my daughter Anna, myself and my husband Andrew. My daughter is 6 years old now and for 2 years she has been asking for a cat as a gift for herself on all holidays. Due to the fact that the war started in our country, we postponed this issue until better times. But for this New year, I decided that it was time and Santa Claus brought us a little fluffy miracle under the Christmas tree. We named him Leo. He is smart, kind and the best friend for my daughter and now a member of our family. If you want to see some Leo's photo please foolow the link.

The Chinchilla British Shorthair

The Chinchilla British Shorthair has a gorgeous white coat with silver highlights. If you’ve ever seen one of these kittens, you’ll be struck by its pretty colors and sweet personality.However, there’s much more to a Chinchilla British Shorthair cat than its looks and temperament. The cat does get along well with other animals, children, and anyone who wants an easy-going, laid-back companion.
